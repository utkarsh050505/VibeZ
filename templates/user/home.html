About Our Space

Our space is designed for innovators, makers, and dreamers. Whether you're building your next prototype or brainstorming the next big idea, VibeZ has you covered.

We provide access to a full suite of electronics components and tools, including Arduino boards, Raspberry Pi kits, sensors, breadboards, jumper wires, resistors, capacitors, ICs, and soldering equipment — all ready to use.

Collaborate effortlessly with whiteboards for ideation and a projector setup perfect for presentations, workshops, or hackathon demos. At VibeZ, we don’t just offer a space — we power your vision with the tools and tech to bring it to life.
